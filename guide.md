# User Guide - Digital Store

Welcome to Digital Store! This guide will help you navigate the platform as a buyer, seller, or administrator.

---

## üõí For Buyers

### Getting Started

1. **Create an Account**

   - Click "Sign Up" in the navigation bar
   - Enter your email and password, or sign in with Google
   - Choose your account type: **Individual (B2C)** or **Business (B2B)**

2. **Browse Products**
   - Visit the Products page to see all available digital products
   - Use category filters: E-books, Templates, Consulting
   - Products show different prices based on your account type (B2B gets bulk pricing)

### Making a Purchase

1. **Add to Cart**

   - Click "Add to Cart" on any product page
   - Select license type if applicable (Personal/Commercial)
   - View your cart by clicking the cart icon

2. **Checkout**

   - Click "Checkout" from your cart
   - Review your order items
   - Pay securely via PayPal
   - You'll receive confirmation once payment is complete

3. **Download Your Products**

   - Go to your Dashboard
   - Find your order in Order History
   - Click "Download" on any digital product
   - Download links expire in 1 hour for security (you can generate new ones anytime)

4. **Get Invoice**
   - From your Dashboard, click "Invoice" on any order
   - A printable invoice will open in a new tab
   - Business accounts see company details included

## üè™ For Sellers

### Becoming a Seller

1. **Apply to Sell**

   - Go to your Dashboard
   - Click "Become a Seller"
   - Fill out your seller profile (username, display name, bio)
   - Set up your PayPal email for payouts

2. **Wait for Approval**
   - Your application will be reviewed by an admin
   - You'll see "Pending" status until approved
   - Once approved, you can start selling!

### Managing Your Store

1. **Access Seller Dashboard**

   - Go to `/seller` from your dashboard
   - View your sales stats: revenue, orders, products

2. **Add Products**

   - Click "Add New Product"
   - Fill in product details:
     - Title and description
     - B2C and B2B pricing
     - Category selection
     - Upload product image
     - Upload digital file for download
   - Save as Draft or Publish

3. **Manage Products**

   - View all your products in the Products tab
   - Edit, archive, or delete products
   - Track views and sales

4. **Customize Your Store Page**
   - Visit `/creators/[your-username]` to see your public store
   - Add custom sections and content
   - Share your store link with customers

### Getting Paid

1. **View Earnings**

   - Check your balance in the Seller Dashboard
   - See breakdown by product

2. **Request Payout**

   - Click "Request Payout" when balance reaches minimum
   - Payouts are sent to your registered PayPal email
   - Admin will process and complete the payout

3. **Commission**
   - Platform takes a small commission (typically 10%)
   - You receive the rest via PayPal

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: How long are download links valid?**
A: Download links expire after 1 hour for security. You can generate new links anytime from your Dashboard.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Digital products are generally non-refundable. Contact seller for specific issues.

**Q: How do I become a seller?**
A: Log in, go to Dashboard, click "Become a Seller", and wait for admin approval.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Currently, we accept PayPal for all purchases and seller payouts.

**Q: How do B2B prices work?**
A: Business accounts see special bulk pricing. Register as B2B to access these rates.

**Q: Is my payment information stored?**
A: No, all payments are handled securely by PayPal. We never see your card details.
